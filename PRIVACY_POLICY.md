# Privacy Policy for Tetris Multiplayer

**Last updated: November 5, 2025**

## Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Tetris Multiplayer mobile application (the "App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Player Name**: You choose a display name when playing the game
- **Game Scores**: Your scores and lines cleared are recorded
- **Game Data**: Game statistics and gameplay information

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Basic device information for app functionality
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app to improve gameplay

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Display your chosen player name during multiplayer games
- Maintain the global leaderboard
- Enable multiplayer functionality
- Improve the game experience
- Ensure proper game operation

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely using Google Firebase services
- Data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard SSL/TLS
- We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information
- Your data is stored on secure Firebase servers

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your information for marketing purposes

Your information is only used within the app for gameplay and leaderboard functionality.

## Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:
- **Firebase Realtime Database**: For multiplayer game synchronization
- **Cloud Firestore**: For storing leaderboard data

These services have their own privacy policies:
- [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

## Data Retention

- Game scores are retained indefinitely on the leaderboard
- Player names are retained as long as they appear on the leaderboard
- You can request data deletion at any time

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new policy in the app
- Updating the "Last updated" date
- Notifying you through the app (for significant changes)
